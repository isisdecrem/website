code.learn()

Coding for middle schoolers

Explore

FOOP

Learn Scheme and Java based off Gunn's FOOP course. This course will teach you the basics of functional and object-oriented programming. If you plan on taking FOOP or APCS in high school this is a great way to prepare yourself.

SWIFT

Learn how to code your very own iPhone app. This course will teach you the basics of Swift, XCode, and Firebase data storage. Students will design apps are part of the curriculum and then at the end of the course design their own app based on their ideas.

Session details

Learning

2 instructors per class. Classes max out at 8 students to ensure a small learning environment. There will be some lecturing, but learning is project based.

Time

Classes will be 3 hours Mon-Fri. A snack break will be included with food provided. Possible time frames are 10am-1pm or 1pm-4pm.

Sessions

Summertime session dates are not yet decided. Each course will last 2 weeks.

About Us

WHO

We are Gunn high school students who became friends through coding. Together we have built an app for Mozilla's spring hackternship, and taken APCS together.

WHERE

We are located in Palo Alto. All our lessons will be locally taught. If you are interested in opening up another branch, please contact us.

WHO

We decided to start Code.learn() to share our love of CS. We both aren't you typical tech nerd and hope to help other kids discover that coding is for everyone.

Charity

The funds we recieve from this class will all go to helping public schools in Tonga. Isis has travelled to Tonga and volunteered at the local schools. When she went in 2018 she was able to collect enough donations to arrange for the shipment of school supplies and sports equiptment to Vava'u. After visiting the schools and seeing the stark contrast between schools there and in America, Isis is committed to continue to bring change.
